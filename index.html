Bug Cross Arcade

Please help Ms. Buggy!

Ms. Buggy is a poor little ladybug that needs to cross the street in safety so she can be with her family.

Unfortunately humans don't pay a lot of attention to little bugs like Ms. Buggy and they tend to step on them, that's why you need to guide her and avoid humans.

The more you help her the more happy she'll be, if you want to know how many times you've made her happy just look at the score!

score: lives:

How to Play

To play just use your keyboard arrows to control Ms. Buggy.

if she gets smashed by a human she loses a life, if she gets out of life she dies and you have to start again.

every time you get her to the other side of the street your score gets up by 1 point.
